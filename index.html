Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, programs, and data from cyber attacks:

  • Devices: Smartphones, laptops, tablets, and computers
  • Services: Online and at work
  • Data: Personal information stored on devices and online

Cybersecurity protects against unauthorized access, theft, damage, and other threats. It's important to safeguard sensitive information, maintain privacy, and prevent finan>

Some examples of cyber attacks include:

  • Accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information
  • Extorting money from users through ransomware
  • Interrupting normal business processes

To protect against cyber attacks, you can:

  • Choose strong passwords
  • Be wary of attachments in email
  • Back up data
